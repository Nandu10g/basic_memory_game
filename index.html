A Simple Memory Game

GAME INSTRUCTIONS

  • In the given 4x4 board there are a total of 16 delicious food images.
  • Out of 16 there are two sets of 8 unique images.
  • You can turn two cards up at a time to check if they are same images.
  • If they selected cards are same you will get two points.
  • Your goal is to empty the board by matching all the pairs on the board (high score = 16 points).
  • Try to get 16 points in least amount of turns.
  • Have FUN!
